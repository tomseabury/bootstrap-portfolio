Here's how the game works

1. The yellow box is given a random number which will be the target number to reach.

2. The blue box will start at 0 and gradually increase each time you click one of the four crystals below

3. Each crystal will be assigned a random number that you will figure out as you click them and watch your score increase

4. You win the game by getting your number to equal what is shown in the yellow box. However, push your number to high, and you will lose!
